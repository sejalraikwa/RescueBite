The Process

Three steps to rescue food

01

Donor lists surplus food

Restaurants, caterers, or households log in and post available food — quantity, type, location, and pickup window.

02

Volunteers get notified

Nearby volunteers see pins appear on the live map. They claim a pickup and head to the donor's location.

03

Food reaches people

The volunteer delivers food to shelters, community kitchens, or directly to those in need. Zero waste.
